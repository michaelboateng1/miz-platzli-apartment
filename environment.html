Sustainability

Built for the Future.

We believe in progress that respects nature. Our Achimota residence integrates cutting-edge green technology to reduce our carbon footprint.

Solar Energy

Harnessing the Ghanaian sun to power 40% of our common area lighting and security systems.

Rainwater Harvest

Integrated filtration systems that recycle rainwater for our botanical gardens and car wash bays.

Smart Glass

Advanced thermal-insulated windows that keep interiors cool, significantly reducing air conditioning costs.
